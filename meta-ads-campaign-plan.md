## 1. Campaign Overview

**Campaign Name:** "Your Keys, Your Way" — Halifax First Home & Renewal Campaign

**One-sentence summary:** Drive Nova Scotia residents to book a free mortgage consultation with Gourav Rai by surfacing programs (DPAP, renewal switch) that their bank will never mention.

**Primary Objective:** Generate qualified consultation form submissions (leads) from first-time buyers and renewal-ready homeowners in Halifax/Dartmouth and surrounding NS communities.

**Secondary Objectives:**
- Build local brand awareness for "Mortgage With Garry" as the go-to broker in NS
- Drive qualified traffic to the landing page for retargeting pool building
- Educate audience on DPAP and no-stress-test renewal switching

## 2. Target Audience

### Audience Segment 1 — First-Time Buyers (Primary)
- **Who:** Halifax-area renters, 25–42 years old, household income $60K–$145K
- **Pain points:** Can't save a down payment fast enough; confused by the mortgage process; don't know what programs they qualify for; worried about getting rejected
- **Motivation:** Homeownership feels closer than they think — they just need someone to lay it out
- **Buying stage:** Consideration to Decision (actively researching, not yet applied)
- **Where they are:** Facebook, Instagram, community groups (HRM Housing, Halifax Rentals), Instagram Reels

### Audience Segment 2 — Renewal / Switch Buyers (Secondary)
- **Who:** Existing NS homeowners, 30–55 years old, mortgage coming up for renewal in 2025–2026
- **Pain points:** Nervous about higher rates; don't realize switching lenders at renewal no longer requires the stress test (since Nov 2024); loyal to their bank out of habit
- **Motivation:** Save money; feel in control of their renewal
- **Buying stage:** Awareness to Consideration (not actively shopping yet, open to being educated)
- **Where they are:** Facebook, Instagram

### Audience Segment 3 — Retargeting Pool (Support)
- **Who:** Anyone who visited the landing page but did NOT submit the form
- **Buying stage:** Decision (high intent, needs a nudge)
- **Angle:** Urgency/social proof — "Gourav reached out to 3 Halifax buyers this week. Rates won't wait."

## 4. Meta Ads Channel Strategy

Meta is the right primary channel because:
- First-time buyers and renewal homeowners in Halifax are highly reachable on Facebook/Instagram
- Mortgage decisions are emotional and visual storytelling via image/video ads works well
- Meta's lead-gen and conversion objective tools map perfectly to the landing page form
- Local geo-targeting keeps spend efficient

### Campaign Structure (3 Ad Sets)

**Ad Set 1 — First-Time Buyer Awareness/Consideration**
- Objective: Traffic → Landing Page (build pixel pool first week), then switch to Conversions
- Audience: Ages 25–42, Halifax/Dartmouth/Truro/Bridgewater, interests: first-time home buyer, renting, real estate, CMHC, down payment, homeownership
- Format: Single image + Carousel (DPAP program explainer)
- Budget signal: ~50–60% of total Meta budget

**Ad Set 2 — Renewal Switcher**
- Objective: Conversions (free consult form submission)
- Audience: Ages 30–55, NS homeowners, interests: mortgage renewal, financial planning, home equity
- Format: Single image or short video (Gourav on camera, 15–30 sec)
- Budget signal: ~25–30% of total Meta budget

**Ad Set 3 — Retargeting**
- Objective: Conversions
- Audience: Landing page visitors (last 30 days), excluding converters
- Format: Single image, urgency + social proof angle
- Budget signal: ~15–20% of total Meta budget
